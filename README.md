## What is Green Tea Powder?

Green tea powder is a finely ground form of specially grown green tea leaves. Unlike regular tea, where leaves are steeped and discarded, green tea powder allows you to consume the entire leaf.

This makes it much more powerful in terms of nutrients and antioxidants. The most popular type of green tea powder is matcha, which is widely used for both drinking and cooking.


## How Does Green Tea Powder Work?
